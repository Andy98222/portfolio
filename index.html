About Me

Developer!

Ciao, I'm Andrea Lombardi, a developer currently based in Turin, Italy. My fascination with computer science and the tech world has been with me since childhood, but it truly blossomed during my school years. That's when I realized that by applying logic and mastering a programming language, I could craft applications to simplify task and automate boring processes, making life easier. My professional journey has been diverse, primarily in ABAP, the proprietary language of the SAP management system. Here, I specialize in developing ERP applications tailored to various business needs, including interfaces, reporting tools, data management solutions, and the creation of documents like invoices and production orders through code. Additionally, I've gained valuable experience in quality assurance, ensuring both functionality and code efficiency in applications. Recently, I've been honing my skills in web development, with a particular emphasis on design that seems to be my weak link for the moment. My curiosity for other branches of computer science has helped me not to isolate myself in the closed world of SAP and has allowed me to stand out in my approach to solutions.
